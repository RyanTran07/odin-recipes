Shrimp Scampi

Description

Shrimp Scampi is a pasta dish that consists of pasta, shrimp, and a light, wine-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Shallots and Garlic
  • White Wine
  • Lemon Juice
  • Seasonings
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Prep ahead
  2. Cook the pasta first
  3. Don't overcook the shrimp
