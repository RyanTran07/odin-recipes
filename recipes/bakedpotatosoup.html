Baked Potato Soup

Description

Baked potato soup is a creamy soup that containms chunky potatoes, bacon, cheese and greenonions on top.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Butter
  • Flour and Milk
  • Baked potatoes
  • Green Onions
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese and Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon
  2. Melt hte butter, then wish in the flour and milk
  3. Add the potatoes and onions and bring to a boil
  4. Reduce to a simmer, then stir in the remaning ingredients
  5. Cook until the cheese is melted
